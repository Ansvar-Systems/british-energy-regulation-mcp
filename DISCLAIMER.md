## Currency and Staleness

- Database updates are **manual** and may lag official publications by weeks or months
- British energy regulation changes through new Acts, Statutory Instruments, licence condition modifications, and regulatory decisions
- A regulation marked "in force" may have been amended or repealed since the last database update
- This Tool does NOT automatically track amendments

## Coverage Gaps

This Tool does **NOT** include:

1. **Full legal text** -- contains representative summaries only
2. **Court decisions** -- Competition Appeal Tribunal and High Court energy rulings are not covered
3. **EU retained energy law** -- post-Brexit retained EU law is not separately tracked
4. **Parliamentary proceedings** -- House of Commons energy debates are not included
5. **Devolved legislation** -- Scottish and Welsh energy legislation is not covered
6. **Historical regulation versions** -- limited to current in-force versions
